Seigr bigård is the apiary in the forest that serves as the main learning source and inspiration for all code development.

It embodies our philosophy of working in harmony with nature, using technology to enhance our understanding of complex systems.

Through observation and experimentation, we aim to create a symbiotic relationship between technology and the natural world, fostering a deeper connection to the environment.

Seigr bigård is also an immense font of inspiration for most of the music published under Antsy Records.
