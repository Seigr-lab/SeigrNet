Antsy Records

The wing responsible for releasing music is Antsy Records. At the moment it is serving as the publisher for the binary and old school distribution platforms. But it will in the future also be the native music distribution for the Seigr Ecosystem.

The most direct way of listening to the catalogue is on Antsy's bandcamp page.

Sonisk Seigr

It is here all the recording, layering, mixing and mastering happens. All the releases published through Antsy Records are produced in this space.

Seigrtronics

This is the wing responsible for designing and developing software related to music creation and/or effects.

At the moment working on a VST plugin/standalone saturation effect called Seig Forvreng.
